# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students and instructors on the CourseMarketplace platform. They use the purchase history to track their orders, download receipts, view purchase details, request refunds, and check refund statuses.

## Product Purpose
CourseMarketplace is a platform for buying and selling online courses. The Purchase History workspace provides users with a clear, reliable ledger of their transactions, refund requests, and invoice records.

## Brand Personality
- Professional and trustworthy (similar to Stripe or Stripe Billing)
- Clean and minimal (similar to Linear or Figma)
- Highly aligned and legible

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, poorly-aligned tables
- Badges that overlap buttons or text
- Inconsistent spacing and margins
- Cliché SaaS dashboard visuals (side-stripe card colors, glowing gradients)

## Design Principles
1. **Absolute Grid Alignment**: Every row must follow a strict columnar layout on desktop so that fields align perfectly across the list.
2. **Intentional Spacing**: Establish a clear hierarchy and rhythm with responsive padding (tighter on mobile, spacious on desktop).
3. **High Contrast Status Cues**: Use clear, readable, and non-overlapping status badges that immediately communicate transaction health.
4. **Distinct Actions**: Separate navigation triggers from status indicators. Make CTAs clear and easy to click.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear contrast ratios for text and background status colors (following WCAG AA).
- Responsive spacing to prevent text clipping at different zoom levels.
